How to Use EchoAnswer

Master the art of seamless interaction with your AI-powered chat assistant.

Getting Started

Step 1: Provide Context

Use the "Context Box" to upload files, enter a URL, or provide a paragraph to give the AI the necessary background.

You can also use the microphone button to input context via voice.

Step 2: Start Chatting

Type your questions in the "Chat Area" and get real-time, accurate responses tailored to your context.

Step 3: Access Previous Chats

Use the sidebar to revisit your previous conversations, manage chats, and continue where you left off.

Step 4: Explore Advanced Features

Customize settings, manage your profile, and integrate EchoAnswer with external services like Drive or OneDrive.
